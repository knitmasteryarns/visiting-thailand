Getting There
NY to Bangkok
You are out of luck if you want to fly direct between NY and Thailand but several airlines offer one stop from NYC to Bangkok. Check out Cathay Pacific, Qatar Airways and Eva Air. If you are open to two or more stop-overs, you can get cheaper fares with China Airlines, China Eastern Airlines and Asiana Airlines.
Getting around Bangkok
The Skytrain, or BTS, is the easiest way of getting around the city. It is cheap and convenient. Click here for fares and other info.
Bangkok to the rest of the country
Thai airways is your best option here. They fly throughout the country primarily from Suvarnabhumi Airport.
