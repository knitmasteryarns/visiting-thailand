Why Thailand?

Thailand has something for everyone. It's a great place to relax and recharge, but you can also find adventure and intrigue. There are unlimited opportunities for island hopping, and everyone knows about the legendary beaches. There's no shortage of luxury hotels, along with budget hotels, hostels and even tree houses! National parks offer a glimpse into the country's biodiversity and unique species. It's a shopper's paradise; you can find pretty much anything. And when you're ready for a break, the street food is quick, easy and delicous. Have a look around and see if Thailand is the place for you.
